If Not “You” then “Who”

If not you then Who! If not now then “When”. If not Pradhaan then “What”.

Still listening to their stories. Them asking you to follow than lead. Repeat their narrative than make your own stories. Media speaking either for party in power or for opposition but not for you, the people of this nation. Babus forgetting that its people who are the real master than the Government of the time. They told you are that you are nothing but “Your Caste”. You owe to none but to your “Your religion”. You are from North, or South. You are Indian or Bhartiya. That all of you, Tribes, Dalits, OBCs, EBCs , will be equal and treated equally. You will have freedom. Not only from atrocities but from hunger and poverty. That none of you, the youth, will be unemployed. None will be poor. That no one will have to sleep hungry. None will be raped or harassed. That your broken roads will be maintained or jammed drain be cleared in no time. That schools will give quality education for all and hospitals will provide affordable healthcare for all. But You chose to remain silent!! Despite cracks on the road was getting larger. Spoke nothing when private hospital charged you your month salary for conducting tests which could have been easily done away with. You hardly said anything when local block officer demanded thousands for creating your own residential certificate. You remained silent when local councilor did nothing when atrocities were committed on your family by local police. Its definitely the time to say “enough is enough” . To understand that democracy is not a democracy without its people. Leaders who don't represent their constituents render democracy hollow. It loses its essence without the voices of its people being heard.

This is where the Pradhaan app comes in, offering you the opportunity to become what you truly deserve by doing what you should do. It's a chance not only to break your silence, but speak up. A chance to Step Up. To be a leader for your community by demanding what your people deserve. A chance to ask questions. Why the neighborhood is not cleaned? Why water supply did not come today? Why electricity fare is high? Speaking about events in your area. What is wrong and what is right. Whether municipality wokers visited today for cleaning your neughbourhood or not. Speak out for women facing harassment, champion the cause of the poor and marginalized, and be the voice for your neighbors, friends, family, and most importantly, yourself. And tell them that you no longer need entertainment in the name of news. You are no longer pleased by election time revadis. That you don’t need promises but actions. And that your votes are not for sale. And Turncoats will not be forgiven. Begin by being the change you wish to see around you. Take that first step by installing the application and joining the "Pradhaan revolution." Promise to yourself that it will be not them but You. It will be now, and it will be through none other than Pradhaan.
